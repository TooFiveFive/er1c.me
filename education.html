Facts about Canovee N.S

Canovee National School is a small countryside primary school located to the east of Macroom.

Canovee National School first opened its doors to the children of Canovee over 150 years ago. Much growth and development has taken place since the early years and the locals are justly proud of the current school building, which can cater for the needs of the 172 pupils.
